Property Description

Industrial land with existing factory buildings and documented infrastructure. Title deed (Chanote), bank valuation report, and sale terms are available for buyer review. The site is located on Setthakit 1 Road with 15.5 metres of direct road frontage and canal frontage (Khlong Tha Rat Prayong) on the western boundary.

The property carries a Ror Ngor 4 (ร.ง. 4) factory operation licence. Zoning is Purple Zone (ผังเมืองสีม่วง), designated for industrial and warehousing use under the Krathum Baen City Plan B.E. 2562. Transfer fees are paid by the seller based on the Land Department appraisal value.

The documented factory layout covers 43 labelled functional areas including three weaving buildings, dyeing rooms, sizing machines, worker dormitories, a cafeteria, wastewater treatment infrastructure, and storage warehouses.
