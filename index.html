About the Property

Thai Fancy Weaving Co., Ltd. is a fully operational textile manufacturing facility set on 11 rai of prime industrial land in Samut Sakhon Province, located directly on Setthakit 1 Road with 15.5 metres of main-road frontage. The compound includes three weaving factories, dyeing facilities, sizing machines, worker dormitories, a cafeteria, wastewater treatment infrastructure, and extensive warehouse storage — 43 distinct functional areas in total.

The property sits in Krathum Baen District's Purple Zone (ผังเมืองสีม่วง), zoned exclusively for industrial and warehousing use. Its most significant asset beyond the physical infrastructure is an active Ror Ngor 4 (ร.ง. 4) factory operation licence — a government permit that can take years and significant capital to obtain from scratch. This licence transfers with the property.

The canal frontage (Khlong Tha Rat Prayong) along the western boundary adds additional utility and character to the site.
